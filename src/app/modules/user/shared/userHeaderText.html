Welcome to Version 1 of TechIreland.org! Hello World from my directive!

  1. Pick which category on the left best suits your organisation - companies (Irish tech company), multinational (tech company founded elsewhere), investor (invests in technology), hubs (accelerators/incubators/coworking/educational/research centres/government bodies)
  2. Search for your organisations name.
  3. If it exists, click “Claim” which will send us your request and we’ll grant approval for you to take ownership of the page.
  4. If it doesn’t exist, click the button starting with “Create a...” in pink at the top right of your screen.
  5. If it says “Requested” beside your company’s name, that means someone else has claimed your profile. If you feel this has been done in error, please email ciaran@startupdublin.com outlining the issue.
