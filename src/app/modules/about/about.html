About TechIreland

What is TechIreland

Maintained by a team of analysts, TechIreland.org is the most comprehensive, public database of innovation from Ireland.

For the first time, complete data on Ireland’s tech ecosystem will be available to everyone.

Disclaimer: Our product and data is currently in beta. We’re busy adding to TechIreland each day and will have comprehensive data by launch in October 2016.

About TechIreland

Why TechIreland Matters for Irish Startups

As a business development tool helping connect Irish startups with international investors, media and global customers.

As a place to learn about investors here and internationally who are active in Ireland.

To identify Ireland based global partners/customers.

As a feeder of up to date data to third party international databases eg. Crunchbase/Mattermark/f6s etc.

About TechIreland

Who Are We

TechIreland is an all-Ireland project initiated by the Office of the Dublin Commissioner for Startups - an independent, not for profit organization based in Dublin that supports and promotes Irish innovation-led, product-based companies.
